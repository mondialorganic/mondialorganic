Holy Basil Dried Leaves

Holy Basil, scientifically known as Ocimum sanctum or Ocimum tenuiflorum, is a sacred herb revered for its medicinal and spiritual significance in various cultures, particularly in India. Commonly referred to as “Tulsi” in Hindi, this aromatic plant belongs to the mint family and has been used for centuries for its numerous health benefits. In India, Holy Basil is considered a symbol of devotion and is often grown near Hindu temples and households.
